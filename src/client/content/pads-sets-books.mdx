A comprehensive range of sizes and paper types are available for single or multi-part forms.

Carbonless paper can be supplied in our standard weights, or the heavier 70gsm, 80gsm and above if required.

Construction can be by tip-glued into sets or pads or wire stapled into books so that one copy can stay in the book if needed.

With the popularity of laser printers it is now possible to obtain laser guaranteed carbonless A4 size paper which we can supply plain or printed, collated into sets ready for you to use as laser sheets.

* Numbering
* Modulus
* Bar Coding
