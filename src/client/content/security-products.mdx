## Cheques

Supplied in continuous sprocket hole punched format and also A4 sheets for use in laser / inkjet printers. Our cheques are guaranteed to conform to the latest APACS (Association for Payment and Clearing Services) standards in all respects to protect you from fraud. Foil holograms can be added for additional security. Bespoke personalised security screen fugitive panels are available if required for distinctive corporate branding.
