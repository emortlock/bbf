## Integrated Card or Labels

If using a Delivery Note why not incorporate a self adhesive label in the delivery address area?

Or you could have a pop out card incorporated in the form which is laser compatible for personalisation.

Also the option of signature strips and security sealing a photograph in place is available.

With lithographic printing of up to 10 colours we can design your form using one of our many integrated products.

We have a selection of plain integral label or card ex stock products available which you can use to design and print your own forms.
