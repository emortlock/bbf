## Tags

Plain or printed, with reinforcing washers or brass eyelets and attachments of string, cotton, calendar cord, wire or elastic. Printed in up to 2 colours each side and numbered. A variety of colours and types of card or tyvek are available. We can also laminate or encapsulate the tag if required.

## Promotional Pop-ups

Self assembling promotional ‘pop-ups’ which leap from their flat form into the desired shape when opened by the recipient. Ideal for that promotion you are planning - unforgettable!

Binding methods - Perfect Binding, Spiral Binding, Comb Binding, Wire Stapling and Pad Gluing.

## Cutting and Creasing

Any shape and size can be cut, including Header Cards with Euro slot holes and even miniature chocolate boxes. We can supply these personalised items to you with Belgian Truffles and Cremes for promotional gifts.

## Large Format Print

Multi coloured posters on a variety of materials in a number of large sizes.
